Trämli Apotheke

  • Summary

The “Trämli Apotheke” is a newly opened Pharmacy in Weil am Rhein. “Trämli” is the swiss word for a streetcar, and the owner wanted to refer to it cause the Store is right next to a Tram Stop, which plays a big role in getting in new customers for him.


By using the circle as a core element of the signet, I wanted to visualize health as a surrounding of our body aswell as the geomatrical shape of a tramstop sign. Before designing the shape of the streetcar, I researched for designs of historic streetcars in the city of Basle and tried to match the modern and clean style I wanted troughout the appearance of the Logo.

  • Colors

For the signet I used the color of the streetcars in the area the pharmacy is located. Aswell the color connects to the greens which are used in the health sector.

  • Typeface

The Typeface I chose for the Logo is Amsi Pro Narrow by Stawix Ruecha. The Font is inspired by the swiss classic Block Berthold Condensed with it’s modernistic look and feel. While developing the Logotype I tried to find a typeface that’s good readable even for People with limited vision or other restrictions of their eyesight.
