Trämli Apotheke

  • —Branding
  • —Logotype

The “Trämli Apotheke” is a newly opened Pharmacy in Weil am Rhein. “Trämli” is the swiss word for a Tram, and the owner wanted to refer to it cause the Store is right next to a Tram Stop, what’s playing a big role for getting new customers right into the store.


By using the circle as a core element, i wanted to visualize health as a surrounding of our body and the act of taking care. Before designing the shape of the Train, I researched for designs od historic Trams from Basle and tried to match the modern style I wanmted troughout the appearance of the Logo.

  • Colors

So I used the color of the Tram in the area the pharmacy is situated, to get the connection to the greens which are used in the pharmaceutical industry.

  • Typeface

The Typeface I chose for the Logo is Amsi Pro Narrow by Stawix Ruecha. The Font is inspired by the swiss classic Block Berthold Condensed with it’s modernistic look. While developing the Logotype I tried to find a typeface that’s also good readable even for People with limited vision or other restrictions of their eyesight.
